Overview:

Led a strategic enterprise initiative to establish a centralized Product Source of Truth system using Sage X3 ERP. The project consolidated fragmented product master data across multiple business units and integrated four downstream operational systems.

Key Achievements:

Parcel Rate Shopping Automation (Carrier API Integration)

Role: Technical Program Manager

Overview:

Designed and implemented an automated shipping rate optimization system that dynamically evaluates carrier pricing from UPS, FedEx, USPS, and Amazon Fulfillment Services. The system integrates real-time rate data to optimize logistics decisions and reduce shipping costs.

Key Achievements:

  • Integrated APIs from four major carriers (UPS, FedEx, USPS, Amazon Fulfillment)
  • Built rate comparison engine with real-time pricing logic
  • Automated carrier selection based on cost, speed, and service level
  • Implemented rate caching and optimization algorithms
  • Generated approximately $250K in annual shipping cost savings
  • Improved logistics decision velocity by ~40%

Business Impact: Significant cost reduction in logistics operations while maintaining or improving service levels to customers.
